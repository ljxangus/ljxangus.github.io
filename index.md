### LIANG Jiaxin
Ph.D. (CUHK), B.E. (CUHK) \\
Email: jiax.l[AT]outlook.com

I earned my Ph.D. in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2021, under the supervision of <a href="http://www.ie.cuhk.edu.hk/people/soung.shtml" class="highlighted">Professor LIEW Soung-Chang</a>. During my doctoral studies, I was a dedicated member of the <a href="http://wireless.ie.cuhk.edu.hk/" class="highlighted">Advanced Wireless System Group</a>.

My academic journey at CUHK began with a B.E. degree in Information Engineering (2015). Prior to my Ph.D., I served as a Research Assistant at the <a href="https://www.inc.cuhk.edu.hk/" class="highlighted">Insitute of Network Coding</a> (INC) and was an undergraduate researcher with <a href="https://mobitec.ie.cuhk.edu.hk/" class="highlighted">MobiTec</a>, specializing in mobile computing and wireless networking. Beyond research, I am passionate about fostering student creativity, having co-founded and served as a curator for the <a href="https://cuhkoil.ie.cuhk.edu.hk/" class="highlighted">CUHK Open Innovation Lab</a> (CUHKOIL).
